Sandman is a hardcore action platformer

The game tells the story about the Sandman, who wanders the world of children's nightmares. His goal is to find and free the child from the trap of game antagonist - Morpheus. The game includes several chapters. Each chapter tells the story about an individual child. All dreams aren't similar to each other, as well as the children aren't the same: everyone has its own hobbies, impressions and fears.

This game is about how Sandman dies hundreds of times trying to save the child. However, death doesn't mean the defeat. Sometimes dying is a way to pass some part of the level.

One of the game's features - an unusual approach to the checkpoint. The player can put the "resurrection flask," where he wants.

Music submits to the tempo of child's heartbeat. Almost all obstacles move to the beat, which the game rhythm presets.

Prototype

16.01.2017

We posted the prototype in open access, because we need the opinion of other people, especially future players. We want to make the game really interesting, based on the reviews.

The prototype has one level, showing the visual style of the coming game, and five levels, showing the gameplay.

This is an early build. So it has bugs and performance issues as in all early builds. We know about it.

The low frame rate directly influences the responsiveness of the control. So if you feel the delay - go to settings. The picture is the part of the fun, of course, but you just wont enjoy the game with poor responsiveness!



We are waiting for your feedback. This will help us to make the game better.
