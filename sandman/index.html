Sandman
in development

Sandman is a hardcore action platformer

The game tells the story of the Sandman, wandering the world of children's nightmares. His goal is to find and free the child from the trap game antagonist's - Morpheus. The game includes several chapters, each chapter tells the story of an individual child. All dreams are not similar to each other, as well as the children aren't same: everyone has his own hobbies, impressions and fears.

This game is about how Sandmen die hundreds times trying to save the child. However, death - it doesn't mean defeat. Sometimes die is a way to pass the some part of the level.

One of the features of the game - an unusual approach to the checkpoint. The player can put the "resurrection flask," where he want.

Music submits to tempo of child's heartbeat. Almost all the obstacles are moving to the beat, which gives the game rhythm.
