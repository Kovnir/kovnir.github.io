Through the Nightmares is a hardcore action platformer

The game tells the story about the Sandman, who wanders the world of children's nightmares. His goal is to find and free the child from the trap of game antagonist - Morpheus. The game includes several chapters. Each chapter tells the story about an individual child. All dreams aren't similar to each other, as well as the children aren't the same: everyone has its own hobbies, impressions and fears.

This game is about how Sandman dies hundreds of times trying to save the child. However, death doesn't mean the defeat. Sometimes dying is a way to pass some part of the level.

The Sandman is helped by the ability to decrease and use the resurrecting flask with magical sand.

Music submits to the tempo of child's heartbeat. Almost all obstacles move to the beat, which the game rhythm presets.
